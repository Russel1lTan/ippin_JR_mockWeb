Japanese restaurant Brisbane

Rooted in tradition and elevated by creativity.

IPPIN offers a refined yet welcoming dining experience inspired by the artistry of Japanese cuisine. From delicate sashimi to soul-warming hot dishes, every plate is crafted with precision and passion by our highly skilled culinary team, led by seasoned chefs with decades of experience.

Our menu showcases premium ingredients, seasonal specialties, and authentic techniques. Guests can explore our curated selection of Japanese sake, unique handcrafted cocktails infused with Japanese elements, and an extensive wine list designed by a Master of Wine.

Whether you're here for an intimate Kaiseki experience, a lively banquet with friends, or a quiet moment over sushi and sake, IPPIN invites you to indulge in the beauty of Japanese dining, where every detail matters.

Welcome to IPPIN, where every dish is a story, and every visit is a memory.
