IPPIN Wine Pairing Night

Hosted by Master of Wine Peter Scudamore-Smith and wine expert Tony Moxon, this intimate experience takes guests through French wine regions including Alsace, Savoie, Burgundy, Rhone, Beaujolais, Cotes de Beaune, Corton, and Cote Rotie.

Each pour is paired with a course from IPPIN's seasonal menu, highlighting the harmony between Japanese cuisine and French terroir.
